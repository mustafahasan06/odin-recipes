Lasanga


Description

Lasagna is very famous Italian dish as it's known with multi-layered cheese that eye eats before mouth and maybe the nose too


ingredient

  • Lasagna papers
  • Cheese
  • Ground meat

Steps

  1. boil the lasagna papers for a few minutes, prepare the meat too
  2. put the lasagna papers that on an oven tray
  3. after the first layer of lasagna papers add your meat
  4. follow the meat with the cheese you have
  5. and the loop continues until you're done with the ingredient.
  6. always make the cheese on the top , then put the tray in the oven
  7. Viola! you have made a very authentic Italian Lasagna!
