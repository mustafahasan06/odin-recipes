Palestinian Molokhia


Description

Molokhia is arguably the tastiest dish in the universe ,but how a green leaf and water make this thing awesome?

Ingredient

  • Molokhia leaf
  • Chicken soup

Steps

  1. put the soup on a pot
  2. wait for the soup to boil
  3. put your molokhia now
  4. after 15 minutes taste it and check the salt
  5. Viola! you have now the tastiest dish in the UNIVERSE!
