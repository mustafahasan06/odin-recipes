Egg and Onion!

Description

Egg and onion might be one of the easiestdishes but some consider it the tastiest proportionally with its ease so we'll see!

Ingredient

  • Egg
  • Onion

Steps

  1. put an oil on pan and fri the diced onion until golden brown
  2. crack some eggs on the onion and mix gently or hardly you're the one to decide!
  3. and after some time...it's finished! your egg and onion ready to get consumed
